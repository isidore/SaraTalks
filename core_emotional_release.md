### Emotional Release

#### 1) Hand Screams
For: **Anger**
Pass both hands over your mouth. Scream into your hands while shaking your body back and forth.

#### 2) Pillow Pounding
For: **Anger**
Kneel in front of two pillows. Put your hands above your head. fists clasped so they don't so nothing will crunch and bend at the hip, striking into the pillow while We're releasing sound, usually a "No".

#### 3) Freeze Release
For: **Freeze**
Curl into a ball while pushing out your breath. Then spring your body flat while taking a breath in. And repeat three times.

#### 4) Grief Wail
For: **Sadness**
Lay on your back with a pillow under your back, your arms to the side. Make a large sound outwards and wail.
